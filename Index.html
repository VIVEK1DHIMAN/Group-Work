About Me

It all started with a fascination for the art of mixology that grew into a burning passion. Years ago, I stepped behind the bar for the first time, and from that moment on, I knew I had found my true calling. Over the years, I've had the privilege of meeting incredible people, creating countless cocktails, and learning valuable lessons along the way.

We believe that bartending is not just about mixing drinks hut also about creatina memorable experiences for our customers.
